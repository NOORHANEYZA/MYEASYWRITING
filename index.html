MY EASY WRITING

My Easy Writing is basically an innovative teaching and learning method specially designed as a value added that enables students to work smarter and faster by attaching the power and efficiency of computer software and mobile application. My Easy Writing is divided into 3 sections: (i) Project Guidelines ; (ii) Effective Writing and (iii) Report Format.These Knowledge and skills will be very useful to improve the students' writing dimension and also helping them in completing their report effectively and systematically..
